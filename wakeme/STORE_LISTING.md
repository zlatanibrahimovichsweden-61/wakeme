## Full description (≤4000 chars)
```
Never miss your stop again. WakeMe is a location-based alarm for commuters,
travellers, and anyone who likes to rest on the move.

Set your destination, choose how far out you want to be woken, and relax.
WakeMe watches your location in the background and rings a full-screen alarm
the moment you get close — even if your phone is locked or the app is closed.

WHY WAKEME
• Sleep, read, or zone out without watching the window.
• Full-screen alarm with sound and vibration you can't sleep through.
• Works on trains, buses, the metro, and long car rides.
• Adjustable wake-up radius (200 m – 2 km) so you get warning, not a jolt.
• Save your home, work, and favourite stops for one-tap trips.
• Clean, dark interface that's easy on tired eyes.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Search for or tap your destination on the map.
2. Pick your wake-up distance.
3. Arm the trip and put your phone away.
4. WakeMe rings as you approach — dismiss and you're done.

PRIVACY FIRST
Your location is used only to power the alarm and is processed on your device.
WakeMe does not sell your data, show ads, or require an account. Location data
is sent securely to mapping services solely to display the map and find places.

Sweet dreams — WakeMe has the wheel.
```
